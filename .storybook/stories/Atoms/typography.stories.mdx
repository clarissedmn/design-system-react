# ✏️ Typography

#

#

#### Typography specifies the fonts that can be use in order to keep a brand's font consistent.

#### It is an essential element of visual identity, carries the messages of the brand and reflects part of its "soul".

#

---

**Fonts** used for our digital product are specified as below (in order of priority):

1. Avenir
2. Century Gothic

---

# Title

To use a title,

1. Specify the `level` of the title between 1 & 4 (corresponding to `h1`, `h2`, `h3` & `h4`)
2. Define its `children`

<Preview>
  <Story name="Title">
    <div>
      <Title level={1}>h1 heading</Title>
      <Title level={2}>h2 heading</Title>
      <Title level={3}>h3 heading</Title>
      <Title level={4}>h4 heading</Title>
    </div>
  </Story>
</Preview>
